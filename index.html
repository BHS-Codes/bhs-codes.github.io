Who We Are

Bonita Codes (BHSC) is a student-led coding club designed to bring programmers of all skill levels together through hands-on coding activities and projects. Whether you've had years of experience or just starting out, come on in!

We meet in Room 301 every first and third Thursday of the month.

What We Do

Learn

Explore computer-centric careers, learn new skills, discuss programming concepts, and find new opportunities to code.

Build

Construct robots, code programs, build websites, and collaborate with other passionate creators.

Play

Participate in hackathons, compete in competitions, win coding challenges and games, and make memories with others.
